About Us

Meet Your Kitchen and Bathroom Remodeling Contractor

My name is Orlando Salinas owner and Founder of CQR Kitchen & Bath Remodeling Specialist. I’ve been kitchen and bathroom remodeling contractor since 1987, well over 32 years. When I started in this industry there was a lack of integrity and quality of work here in the RGV. Homeowners were getting the bad end of the deal and many contractors were not delivering what they promised. No one really specialized in anything, including myself. I decided to take my chances and start a remodeling company that would specialize exclusively in kitchen and bath renovations and build a team of experts that people could trust. And in 2003 CQR Kitchen & Bath Remodeling Specialist was born!

Redefining Quality and Service

When Orlando started CQR, he knew finding a kitchen and bathroom remodeling contractor you can trust was not easy. Thats why he decided to focus on building a company that specialized in kitchen and bathroom renovation while serving his customers with unforgettable customer service.Today, CQR’s hard work and efforts have been able to assemble a team of expert trades men that we believe are the best in the industry here in the RGV. Custom renovating your master bath or kitchen takes skilled trades men and an experienced contractor to put it all together. With great confidence we can tell you that you’ve come to the right place because CQR has the right people. The right cabinet men, the right painters, the right quartz installers, the right tile men, the right plumbers and electricians. We pride ourselves in quality work, quality service, with people you can trust, guaranteed! Please look us up and check out all our 5 star reviews on Google, Angieslist, Facebook and the BBB. We look forward to hearing from you and appreciate the opportunity to show you what we can do.

We Give Your Home the Care it Deserves

When doing a kitchen and bath renovation in your home there’s more to consider than just making sure the job gets done right. Naturally, remodeling requires workers going in and out of your home, carrying large equipment and carrying in materials. Things can get messy quickly. CQR knows how important it is that the rest of your home stays looking just as good as before the job so your can fully appreciate your dream kitchen and bath makeover. To protect your home, our team uses protective plastic to block off areas of your home to keep dust and debris from getting into unwanted areas of the home. In addition, we use masking tape and durable industrial paper to cover the remodeling work area floor. Two man teams are used to carry large equipment and material to ensure the safety of walls and door frames. Lastly, we make sure the work area is clean and pristine so you can admire every detail of your new kitchen and bath remodel.
